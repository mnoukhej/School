About Us

Solpara High School Campus

Solpara High School, established in 2005, is a prestigious Bengali and Urdu medium institution located in Solpara, Goalpokhar. We provide quality education from Class V to XII, with a special focus on Arts stream in higher secondary. Our dual-medium approach ensures students excel in both Bengali and Urdu languages while receiving comprehensive education.
